Personal Mission

As an aspiring technologist, my personal mission is to leverage the power of innovation and technology to create meaningful change in the world. Guided by a deep-seated commitment to continuous learning, collaboration, and ethical leadership, I aim to contribute to the advancement of society through my work. I believe in the transformative potential of technology to address pressing global challenges and improve the quality of life for individuals and communities worldwide.

Motivation

Driven by my commitment to addressing the aforementioned need, I founded a grassroots initiative called “Holberton School”.

Collaboration

Holberton School strives to inspire a love for technology, cultivate problem-solving abilities, and prepare young minds for future academic and professional pursuits.

In my community

I have identified a pressing need for accessible technology educationn, particularly among underprivileged youth. Recognizing the transformative power of digital literacy, I am passionate about creating opportunities for young people to develop essential technical skills. By bridging the digital divide and empowering the next generation with technological knowledge, I aim to open doors to new possibilities and equip individuals with the tools they need to thrive in the digital age.

Embracing the core values of integrity, empathy, and resilience, I am dedicated to pursuing excellence and making a positive impact in all my endeavors. My goal is to start a non-profit for global tech education and innovation. I want to create tech hubs and programs that reach people across borders and backgrounds, giving them access to the best learning resources.

Looking ahead

i hope to inspire and empower diverse tech talents who can solve the world’s problems and create a better future.
